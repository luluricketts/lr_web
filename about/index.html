About me

Hey! I'm Lulu and I'm an undergraduate student at UC San Diego studying Cognitive Science (specializing in Machine Learning) and minoring in Computer Science. I aim to use human intelligence as a basis for delivering meaningful AI solutions that will positively impact various communities.

my passions

sustainability

education

Sustainability, whether it be through autonomous electric vehicles that will reduce our carbon emissions or tracking our energy consumption, is crucial to slowing down the effects of climate change, and there is much potential for AI to take on these issues. My interest in education arose after I volunteered as an educator in Taiwan and Tanzania, and seeing the disparities in the quality of education many children around the world receive motivated me to wonder what can be done. Through software and AI, I hope to play a role in combatting these and similar issues.

my skills

Data Science

Many of my projects have been data science/machine learning related and mainly done in Python working with pandas, numpy, sklearn, and visualization tools such as seaborn, matplotlib, and plotly. I've worked with various probability distribution and estimation methods (including Bayesian inference) and worked with deep learning libraries Pytorch and Tensorflow as well.

Software/Web

Though I have some experience in Java/C/C++ for backend development, I like working on some web development as well (like this website!) using HTML/CSS/Javascript and some ReactJS. Research
